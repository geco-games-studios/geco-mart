Frequently Asked Questions

You can track your order by logging into your account and navigating to the "Orders" section. There, you'll find a list of all your orders and their current status. Alternatively, you can use the tracking number provided in your shipping confirmation email to track your package directly on the carrier's website.

We offer a 30-day return policy for most products. To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging. Some products, such as perishable goods, intimate items, and downloadable software, are not eligible for return. Please see our full return policy for more details.

Shipping times vary depending on your location and the shipping method selected at checkout. Standard shipping typically takes 3-7 business days within the continental United States. Expedited shipping options are available for faster delivery. International shipping may take 7-21 business days, depending on the destination country and customs processing.

We accept a variety of payment methods to make your shopping experience convenient. These include major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. For select items, we also offer financing options through our partner services. All payment information is securely processed and encrypted.

To reset your password, click on the "Account" icon in the top right corner of the website, then select "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter the email address associated with your account, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password. If you don't receive the email, please check your spam folder or contact our support team for assistance.
